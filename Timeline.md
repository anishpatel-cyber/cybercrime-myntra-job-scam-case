## Outcome

- No financial transaction was made.
- No registration was completed.
- No banking or payment credentials were disclosed.
- No OTPs were shared.
- No remote access software was installed.
- Evidence was preserved in its original form.
- Incident documented for forensic analysis and reporting.

---

## Observed Social Engineering Techniques

During the interaction, the following tactics were observed:

- Brand impersonation (Myntra)
- Work-from-home employment lure
- Migration from Instagram to WhatsApp
- Promise of unusually high commissions
- Small advance payment request (₹200)
- Psychological commitment through low initial investment
- Use of alleged payment screenshots as social proof
- Continued persuasion after refusal
- Registration bonus to encourage account creation
- Use of urgency and reassurance to reduce suspicion
